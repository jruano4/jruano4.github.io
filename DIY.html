Matcha Recipes

There are many ways to enjoy a cup of matcha, first let's start with a classic matcha latte. Then let's explore how switching ingridents and add ons can make a whole new matcha latte experience. However we need to start with the basics, so let's get into the classic matcha latte recipe.

Classic Matcha Latte

Ingredients:

  • teaspoon-tablespoon of matcha powder
  • Choice of milk for 8oz cup
  • Ice
  • Choice of sweetener(In my case honey is the way to go!)

Steps:

  1. Start by getting your bowl, a sifter, bamboo wisk or frother, for the bamboo wisk you need to get a small cup with some hot water and have the wisk soak in the warmed water for a couple mintues before it's used.
  2. Put the sifter on top of the bowl, then scoop the matcha powder on top and make the powder clump free. Add a little of warm water and honey/sweetner to the bowl.
  3. Then get the wisk and move in a up and dowm motion until it's frothy and bubbles appear. Or grab the frother and make the matcha liquid frothy.
  4. Grab your cup put your desired amount of ice into it then fill it up with your choice of milk. Leave enough room for the matcha liquid.
  5. Now pour your matcha ontop of the milk, then stir and enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

You're able to switch up the matcha latte by adding different ingredients. For example, you can add strawberry puree, bannana puree, or try out another fruit puree. You can also experiment with different types of milk, such as almond, oat, or coconut milk, to find your favorite combination.
